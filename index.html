Welcome to St. Augustine's Major Seminary Jos

Raising Faithful & Disciplined Leaders

St Augustine’s Major Seminary, Jos, is a center of spiritual, academic, and pastoral formation dedicated to nurturing men called to serve God and His people. For decades, the seminary has been committed to providing holistic formation integrating prayer, community life, and rigorous academic study.

Our mission is to form well-rounded priests who are spiritually mature, intellectually equipped, and pastorally competent. At SAMS, seminarians are guided through a structured program of theological education, pastoral training, and personal spiritual development.

Our Mission

St. Augustine’s Major Seminary is dedicated to the holistic formation of future priests — spiritually, intellectually, pastorally, and humanly. Our mission is to nurture men of faith and integrity who will serve the Church and society with compassion, wisdom, and unwavering dedication.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of excellence in priestly formation, inspiring generations of leaders who embody Christ’s love and truth. We envision a seminary community that radiates service, fosters dialogue, and contributes to the renewal of the Church and the transformation of society.

Holistic Formation at SAMS

At the heart of priestly formation lies the Holy Spirit, who unites the human, academic, spiritual, and pastoral dimensions into one vocation. None is greater than the other, and if one is missing, formation is incomplete. Each draws strength from the Spirit, showing that vocation is a divine call — not chosen by oneself, but given and sustained by God.

Answer the Call

Some calls are louder than words — they stir the soul, reshape dreams, and point the heart toward a greater purpose. The call to the priesthood is one such invitation: a call to live for God, to serve His people, and to become a shepherd of faith, hope, and love.

The seminary is where this divine call is nurtured through prayer, study, discipline, and brotherhood. Admission is not just entry into a school — it is the bold first step into a life of meaning, sacrifice, and eternal impact.
