A Message from Our Rector

Welcome to St. Augustine’s Seminary. Here, we are committed to forming men of faith, discipline, and service. Our mission is to nurture seminarians intellectually, spiritually, and pastorally, preparing them to serve the Church with dedication and humility. Together with our faculty and staff, we strive to uphold the Seminary’s tradition of excellence in priestly formation.

— Rev. Fr. [Rector’s Name], Rector
