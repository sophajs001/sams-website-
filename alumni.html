Our Seminary Alumni

Formed in faith, serving the Church and society

Discover More

The alumni of St. Augustine’s Seminary embody the mission of priestly formation, carrying the values of faith, discipline, and service into their ministries and communities. Through years of rigorous academic, spiritual, and pastoral training, our graduates are prepared to lead with humility and dedication. They serve as pastors, educators, and leaders, continuing the Seminary’s legacy of excellence. The alumni network remains a vital part of our community, fostering fellowship and supporting the ongoing mission of the Church.
